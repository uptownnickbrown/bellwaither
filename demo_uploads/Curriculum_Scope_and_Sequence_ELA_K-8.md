## Cross-Cutting Literacy Practices (All Grades)

1. **Independent Reading:** 20+ minutes daily across all grades; reading logs maintained K-8
2. **Read Aloud:** Daily in K-4; weekly in 5-8
3. **Word Study:** Systematic vocabulary instruction aligned to grade-level texts; Tier 2 academic vocabulary emphasis
4. **Writing Process:** All major writing assignments go through planning, drafting, revising, editing, and publishing stages
5. **Newsela:** Grades 3-8 engage with current events articles at adjusted reading levels weekly
6. **Lexia Core5:** K-5 students use adaptive reading technology 3x/week during intervention/enrichment block
